# OMNI-SECTOR ARCHETYPE MATRIX (SFAP v4.0)

This matrix identifies the Top 30 most frequent and impactful SME archetypes across Morocco, Europe, and the World. It serves as the bottom-up foundation for the SFAP Cognitive Synthesizer.

## I. Sectoral Distribution (The Fact Base)

| Region | Primary Sector | Secondary Sector | Tertiary Sector | Volume Marker |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Morocco (HCP)** | Trade/Commerce (36%) | BTP/Real Estate (19%) | Services (18%) | 99% Micro/SME |
| **Europe (Eurostat)** | Services (80%) | Trade (18%) | Construction (12%) | 99.8% SMEs |
| **Global (OECD/WB)** | Retail/Wholesale | Professional Services | Manufacturing | 90% of Businesses |
