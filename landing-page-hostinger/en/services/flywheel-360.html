Is This For You?

Flywheel 360 isn't for everyone. Here's how to know.

Ideal Client

  • Minimum revenue €7k/month (E-commerce) or €20k/month (SMB)
  • Ready to invest in a 12-week transformation
  • Team able to provide access and validate
  • Long-term vision, not just quick-wins
  • Existing data to leverage (customers, sales, leads)
  • Commitment to the process

Not For You If

  • Startup without traction (Revenue < €5k/month)
  • Looking for immediate results without investment
  • No time to dedicate to the project
  • Tight budget, cannot wait 12 weeks
  • Prefer "magical" solutions without effort
  • Don't believe in automation
