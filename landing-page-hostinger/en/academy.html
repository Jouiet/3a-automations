What is an automation?

An automation is a task that runs automatically when a condition is met. For example: a customer abandons their cart → an email is sent automatically 1 hour later to remind them. You no longer need to do it manually.

What is an AI agent?

An AI agent goes further than automation. It can analyze, decide, and act. For example: analyze an incoming email, understand the customer's intent, and respond appropriately. 3A Automation uses 22 specialized agents that work together.
