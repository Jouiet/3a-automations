Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from our clients.

How do I access the Academy?

The Academy is reserved for clients with an active pack (Quick Win, Essentials or Growth). After subscribing, you'll receive your login credentials by email. If you're already a client and haven't received access, contact our support.

Are courses updated regularly?

Yes, we update content monthly to reflect new features and market best practices. You'll be notified by email when new content is available.

Can I download courses for offline viewing?

Quick guides are available as downloadable PDFs. For video courses, streaming is required to ensure you access the latest version of the content.

How do I get help if I'm stuck?

Several options: use the support chat accessible from the dashboard, browse our knowledge base, or schedule a call with our team (included in Essentials and Growth packs).

How long does it take to complete a learning path?

Each path shows its estimated duration (3-6 hours on average). You can progress at your own pace - your progress is saved automatically. Most clients complete a path in 1-2 weeks.
