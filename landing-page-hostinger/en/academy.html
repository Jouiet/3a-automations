Mastering the Dashboard
4 lessons · 45 minutes
1.1 Logging in and navigating

Login steps

  1. Open dashboard.3a-automation.com
  2. Enter your credentials (email + password provided)
  3. Click Sign in

The dashboard displays:

  • Real-time metrics

    Today's leads, MTD revenue, active workflows, conversion rate

  • Leads by source chart

    LinkedIn, Meta Ads, Google Ads, Organic, Referral (last 30 days)

  • Alerts and notifications

    Leads not contacted >24h, workflow status, remaining API credits

1.2 Managing your leads

Lead pipeline

Each lead goes through these statuses:

  1. New → Freshly captured lead
  2. Contacted → First contact made
  3. Qualified → Need confirmed, budget validated
  4. Proposal → Quote sent
  5. Won / Lost → Final result

Quick actions on a lead:

  • Send an email (click email icon)
  • Send a WhatsApp (click phone icon)
  • Add a note
  • Change status
  • Assign to team member
1.3 Reading analytics

Key metrics to monitor

  • Leads/week — Target: 50+ for active SMB
  • Email open rate — Target: >50%
  • Response rate — Target: >5%
  • Conversion rate — Target: >2%

Charts refresh automatically every 30 seconds.

Configuring Klaviyo
5 lessons · 60 minutes
2.1 Creating a Welcome Flow

Prerequisites

  • Admin access to Klaviyo account
  • Duration: 30 minutes

Step 1: Access Klaviyo

  1. Log in to klaviyo.com
  2. Navigate to Flows in the sidebar
  3. Click Create Flow
  4. Select Create from Scratch
  5. Name the flow: Welcome Series - 5 Emails

Step 2: Configure the Trigger

  1. In the trigger panel, select Metric
  2. Search and select: welcome_series_started
  3. Configuration: Trigger Once per person

Step 3: The 5 emails in the sequence

  • Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome — Immediate send
  • Email 2 (Day 2): Social Proof — Client results
  • Email 3 (Day 4): Best Sellers — Top 3 automations
  • Email 4 (Day 7): Educational — Complete guide
  • Email 5 (Day 14): Offer — 10% promo code

Flow Structure

[TRIGGER: welcome_series_started] ↓ [EMAIL 1: Welcome] ↓ [DELAY: 2 days] ↓ [EMAIL 2: Social Proof] ↓ [DELAY: 2 days] ↓ [EMAIL 3: Best Sellers] ↓ [DELAY: 3 days] ↓ [EMAIL 4: Educational] ↓ [DELAY: 7 days] ↓ [EMAIL 5: Offer]
2.2 Testing the Flow

Test command

curl -X POST "https://n8n.srv1168256.hstgr.cloud/webhook/subscribe/new" \ -H "Content-Type: application/json" \ -d '{ "email": "test-flow@youremail.com", "first_name": "TestFlow", "source": "klaviyo-test" }'

Verification

  1. Profile created in Klaviyo → Profiles
  2. Event welcome_series_started visible on profile
  3. Email 1 queued/sent in Flow → Analytics

4.1 System architecture
LEAD SOURCES │ ┌────────┼────────┐ ▼ ▼ ▼ LinkedIn Google Meta Scraping Ads Ads │ ▼ n8n ENRICHMENT + AI SCORING │ ┌────────┼────────┐ ▼ ▼ ▼ Email LinkedIn WhatsApp Sequence Connect Message │ ▼ CRM DASHBOARD
4.2 LinkedIn Scraper

Configuration

  • Query: "E-commerce Manager" OR "Digital Marketing"
  • Location: Morocco, France, Belgium, Canada
  • Company size: 11-200 employees
  • Industry: Retail, Fashion, Beauty, Consumer Goods

Output

  • Name, Title, Company
  • LinkedIn URL
  • Work email (enriched)
  • Score (0-100)

Frequency: Every 6 hours · Limit: 100 profiles/run

4.3 AI Lead Scoring

Scoring criteria (100 points)

  • Title match (E-commerce, Marketing, Growth) → +30 pts
  • Company size (11-200) → +20 pts
  • Industry match (Retail, E-commerce) → +20 pts
  • Location (Morocco, France) → +15 pts
  • Engagement signals → +15 pts

Thresholds

  • Hot (80-100): Immediate outreach
  • Warm (50-79): Nurturing sequence
  • Cold (<50): Low priority
4.4 Multi-Channel Outreach Sequence

Outreach timeline

  • Day 0: LinkedIn connection request (personalized)
  • Day 1: Connection accepted → Welcome message
  • Day 3: Value-add content (case study)
  • Day 5: Email outreach (if email available)
  • Day 7: Follow-up email
  • Day 10: WhatsApp (if phone available)
  • Day 14: Final follow-up
