Security

Your Data, Your Control

You Remain the Owner

Your accounts (Shopify, CRM, GA4, etc.) belong to you. We operate with delegated access revocable at any time.

Minimal Permissions

Principle of least privilege: we only request necessary access, never full admin.

Strict Isolation

Each client has their isolated environment. No credential sharing between clients.

Regular Rotation

Credentials and tokens renewed every 60-90 days according to security best practices.

Complete Audit Trail

All actions are tracked. You can verify who did what and when.

Immediate Revocation

End of contract? You revoke our access with one click. No data remains with us.
