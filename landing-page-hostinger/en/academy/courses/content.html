Content

Automated Content

1h 10min
6 lessons

Leverage AI to generate blog posts, newsletters, and social content while maintaining your brand voice.

1 AI Content Fundamentals 12 min

Understand how AI content generation works and where it fits in your workflow.

Key Principles

  • AI as assistant - Not replacement, but accelerator
  • Human review - Always edit before publishing
  • Brand voice - Train the system on your style
  • Fact-checking - Verify claims and statistics
2 Blog Post Generation 15 min

Generate SEO-optimized blog posts from topics, keywords, or competitor analysis.

Blog Generation Process

Workflow

Content> Blog Generator> Enter topic and keywords> Generate outline> Review> Generate full post> Edit and publish.

Best Practice

Start with outline generation. A good structure leads to better content. Edit the outline before generating the full post.

3 Newsletter Automation 12 min

Automate weekly or monthly newsletters with curated content and AI-generated summaries.

Newsletter Components

  • Intro - Personal touch from you
  • Featured content - Your best blog/video
  • Curated links - Industry news
  • CTA - Product or service spotlight
4 Social Media Content 10 min

Repurpose blog posts into social media content for LinkedIn, Twitter, and Instagram.

5 Product Descriptions 10 min

Generate and optimize product descriptions at scale for your e-commerce store.

Description Generation

Input

Product name, features, target audience, tone. Output: Multiple description variants for A/B testing.

6 Content Calendar 11 min

Plan and schedule content across channels with automated publishing.

Calendar Setup

In the Dashboard

Content> Calendar> Plan posts> Set publish dates> Connect channels> Automate publishing.
