Email Marketing

Emails That Convert

1h 30min
8 lessons

Create high-performing email sequences: welcome, abandoned cart recovery, dormant customer reactivation.

1 Email Marketing Fundamentals 10 min

Before diving into sequences, understand the core principles of effective email marketing.

Key Metrics

  • Open Rate - % who open your email (target:>20%)
  • Click Rate - % who click a link (target:>2%)
  • Conversion Rate - % who take desired action
  • Unsubscribe Rate - Keep under 0.5%
2 Welcome Sequence 12 min

First impressions matter. A strong welcome sequence sets expectations and drives first purchases.

Welcome Sequence Design

Recommended Structure

Email 1 (immediate): Welcome + brand story. Email 2 (day 1): Top products. Email 3 (day 3): Social proof. Email 4 (day 5): First-purchase offer.

3 Abandoned Cart Recovery 15 min

Recover 10-15% of abandoned carts with the right sequence.

Cart Recovery Best Practices

Tip

Don't offer a discount in the first email. Many customers will complete without incentive.

4 Post-Purchase Emails 12 min

The relationship doesn't end at purchase. Build loyalty with thoughtful follow-up.

5 Win-Back Campaigns 10 min

Re-engage customers who haven't purchased in 60-90 days.

6 Subject Line Best Practices 10 min

Your subject line determines if the email gets opened. Learn what works.

7 Personalization 12 min

Go beyond "Hi {first_name}". Learn to personalize based on behavior and preferences.

8 A/B Testing 9 min

Continuously improve through systematic testing.
