Quick Guides

Learn in 15 minutes or less. 8 practical guides to solve specific problems. Click on a guide to expand it.

Connect Klaviyo

5 min read

This guide shows you how to connect your Klaviyo account to 3A Automation to sync contacts and automations.

Prerequisites

  • An active Klaviyo account
  • Administrator rights on the account

Steps

1. Get your Klaviyo API key

In Klaviyo, go to Account> Settings> API Keys. Click "Create Private API Key". Name it "3A Automation" and select "Full Access".

2. Copy the key

Copy the private key (starts with "pk_"). Note: it will only be displayed once.

3. Add in 3A Automation

In your 3A dashboard, go to Integrations> Klaviyo. Paste the API key and click "Connect".

4. Verify the connection

A confirmation message appears. Your Klaviyo lists are now visible in the interface.

Security tip

Never share your API key. If compromised, delete it in Klaviyo and create a new one.

Connect Shopify

5 min read

Connect your Shopify store to sync products, orders, and customers.

Steps

1. Access the installation

In your 3A welcome email, click the Shopify installation link. Or in your Shopify admin, search for "3A Automation" in the App Store.

2. Authorize access

Shopify displays the requested permissions: read products, orders, customers. Click "Install app".

3. Initial synchronization

The first sync may take a few minutes depending on your catalog size. A progress indicator is displayed.

4. Verification

In 3A> Integrations> Shopify, the status should indicate "Connected" with the last sync date.

Create an email sequence

10 min read

Create an automated sequence that sends multiple emails at defined intervals.

Steps

1. Create a new automation

Automations> Create. Give it a descriptive name (e.g., "Welcome new subscribers").

2. Define the trigger

Choose the event that starts the sequence: list subscription, purchase, abandoned cart, etc.

3. Add the first email

Click "+ Add step"> "Email". Write the subject and content. The first email is sent immediately after the trigger.

4. Add a delay

Click "+ Add step"> "Delay". Set the time before the next email (e.g., 1 day, 3 days).

5. Repeat for each email

Add delay + email for each message in the sequence. 3 to 5 emails are recommended.

6. Activate the sequence

Click "Activate". The sequence starts for new triggers.

Tip

Test first with your own email. In settings, add your address as "Test Email" to receive previews.

Read your analytics reports

8 min read

Understand the metrics displayed in your dashboard and use them to make decisions.

Main metrics

  • Open rate: % of recipients who opened the email. Target:> 20%
  • Click rate (CTR): % of opens that clicked. Target:> 2%
  • Attributed revenue: Sales generated by your automations
  • Conversion: % of leads who became customers

Interpreting data

  • Low opens → Improve your email subject lines
  • Low clicks → Improve content and CTAs
  • Low conversion → Review your offer or targeting
Tip

Always compare to your own history rather than "industry averages". Every audience is different.

Segment your contacts

12 min read

Create segments to send targeted messages to different groups of contacts.

Steps

1. Access segments

Contacts> Segments> Create a segment.

2. Define conditions

Add rules: "Purchased in last 30 days", "Average cart> $100", "Hasn't opened email in 90 days".

3. Combine conditions

Use AND to accumulate (more restrictive) or OR to expand.

4. Preview

The number of matching contacts is displayed. Verify it's consistent before saving.

Recommended segments

  • VIP: 3+ orders OR total revenue> $500
  • At risk: Purchased 60-90 days ago AND hasn't opened email in 30 days
  • New: Subscribed in the last 7 days

Recover abandoned carts

15 min read

Set up an automated sequence to recover 10-15% of abandoned carts.

3-email strategy

Email 1 - After 1 hour

Simple reminder: "You forgot something". Show cart products with images. No discount yet.

Email 2 - After 24 hours

Add urgency: "These items are in high demand". Include a customer review of a product in the cart.

Email 3 - After 72 hours

Last attempt with incentive: free shipping or 10% off. Create a sense of last chance.

Configuration

  • Trigger: "Checkout Started" (cart created)
  • Exclusion condition: "Placed Order" (completed purchase)
  • Dynamic block: Cart products with images
Tip

Don't offer a discount in the first email. Many customers will complete without incentive - protect your margins.

Customize your templates

10 min read

Adapt email templates to your brand guidelines.

Customizable elements

  • Logo: Upload your logo in the header
  • Colors: Primary color, buttons, links
  • Typography: Font family
  • Footer: Legal information, social networks

Steps

1. Access design settings

Settings> Branding> Email templates.

2. Upload your logo

Recommended format: transparent PNG, width 200-400px.

3. Set your colors

Enter HEX codes from your brand: primary, secondary, buttons.

4. Preview and save

Check rendering on desktop and mobile before validating.

Configure notifications

5 min read

Receive alerts on important events: new leads, sales, errors.

Notification types

  • New lead: When a prospect enters the system
  • Qualified lead: When a lead exceeds the scoring threshold
  • Attributed sale: When an automation generates a sale
  • Error: When an automation fails
  • Low stock: When a product reaches the alert threshold

Available channels

  • Email: Notifications by email
  • Slack: Messages in a dedicated channel
  • Dashboard: Notification badge in the interface
Configuration

Settings> Notifications. Enable/disable each type. For Slack, add your webhook URL.

Tip

Only enable actionable notifications. Too many notifications = you'll ignore them all.
