Marketing Automation

For marketing managers

Master B2B lead generation, personalized email sequences, and advanced analytics to drive your acquisition. This path covers the complete funnel from lead capture to conversion.

~6 hours
8 modules
1

Lead Sources Configuration

45 min

Configure your different lead sources: forms, LinkedIn, Google Maps, partner referrals. Each source feeds your database with contextual data.

You'll be able to

  • Connect form capture tools
  • Set up LinkedIn and Google Maps integrations
  • Tag leads by source for accurate attribution
2

Lead Scoring

45 min

Score your leads based on profile (demographics) and behavior (engagement). Focus your efforts on the hottest prospects.

You'll be able to

  • Define scoring criteria adapted to your business
  • Automate score calculation
  • Create sales alerts when a lead reaches threshold
3

Nurturing Sequences

50 min

Create email sequences that educate and engage your prospects throughout their buying journey. Learn the best practices for B2B nurturing.

You'll be able to

  • Design multi-week nurturing sequences
  • Personalize content based on segment
  • Set up triggers based on behavior
4

Behavioral Segmentation

40 min

Segment beyond demographics: by content consumed, email engagement, and website visits. Create hyper-targeted campaigns.

You'll be able to

  • Create behavior-based segments
  • Trigger automations on engagement signals
  • Personalize web experience based on segment
5

Sales Handoff

35 min

Bridge marketing and sales. Automate qualified lead notifications, CRM sync, and meeting booking.

You'll be able to

  • Define MQL to SQL criteria
  • Create instant sales alerts
  • Automate CRM data enrichment
6

Webinar & Event Automation

45 min

Maximize event ROI with complete automation: registration, reminders, follow-up, replay distribution.

You'll be able to

  • Create a pre-event nurture sequence
  • Automate reminder emails
  • Segment post-event follow-up by attendance
7

Multi-Channel Campaigns

45 min

Orchestrate email, LinkedIn, and SMS for maximum impact. Learn channel selection and timing best practices.

You'll be able to

  • Design cross-channel sequences
  • Respect channel-specific constraints
  • Measure multi-touch attribution
8

Analytics & ROI

40 min

Measure marketing impact on revenue. Build dashboards that prove ROI and guide optimization.

You'll be able to

  • Configure revenue attribution
  • Create executive-ready dashboards
  • Identify optimization opportunities
