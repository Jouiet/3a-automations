Optimized E-commerce

For online store owners

Learn to automate your Shopify store, recover abandoned carts, and increase your average order value with smart email marketing. This path guides you step by step through proven automations.

~4 hours
6 modules
1

Connect Your Store

30 min

In this first module, you'll connect your Shopify store to 3A Automation. We'll sync your products, orders, and customers to enable all subsequent automations.

You'll be able to

  • Install the Shopify app
  • Verify product synchronization
  • Check that events are being received
2

Abandoned Cart Sequence

45 min

Recover 10-15% of abandoned carts with a 3-email sequence that starts gentle and builds urgency. Learn the best timing and content for each message.

You'll be able to

  • Configure the "Checkout Started" trigger
  • Create 3 strategic follow-up emails
  • Set up A/B testing on subject lines
3

Welcome Sequence

40 min

The first impression matters. Create a welcome sequence that transforms new subscribers into buyers with an educational and engaging approach.

You'll be able to

  • Create a 4-5 email welcome flow
  • Personalize based on acquisition source
  • Strategically include a first-purchase offer
4

Post-Purchase & Loyalty

45 min

After purchase comes loyalty. Create flows that thank your customers, request reviews, and encourage repeat purchases.

You'll be able to

  • Create a thank you sequence after purchase
  • Automate review requests
  • Set up cross-sell based on purchase history
5

Customer Segmentation

35 min

Learn to segment your customers based on purchase behavior, order value, and recency to send hyper-personalized messages.

You'll be able to

  • Create VIP, at-risk, and dormant segments
  • Adapt messages based on segment
  • Set up automated win-back flows
6

Measure and Optimize

35 min

Identify what's working and what needs improvement. Learn to interpret KPIs and make data-driven decisions.

You'll be able to

  • Read and understand performance reports
  • Calculate attributed revenue per automation
  • A/B test to continuously improve
