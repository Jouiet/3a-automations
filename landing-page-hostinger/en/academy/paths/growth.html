Accelerated Growth

For founders and executives

Strategic view of automation. Learn to identify growth levers, prioritize high-impact automations, and measure results. This executive-level path gives you the insights to make informed decisions.

~3 hours
5 modules
1

Identify Automation Opportunities

35 min

Audit your current processes to identify the highest-value automation opportunities. Learn a framework for prioritizing what to automate first.

You'll be able to

  • Map your customer journey touchpoints
  • Identify manual processes with high automation potential
  • Estimate ROI for each automation opportunity
2

Prioritize by ROI

40 min

Not all automations deliver equal value. Learn to calculate expected ROI and sequence implementations for maximum impact.

You'll be able to

  • Calculate cost vs. benefit for each automation
  • Create an implementation roadmap
  • Set realistic expectations for results
3

Key Performance Indicators

35 min

Define the right KPIs for your automation strategy. Learn to build dashboards that give you real-time visibility into performance.

You'll be able to

  • Select meaningful KPIs for each automation
  • Build an executive dashboard
  • Set benchmarks and goals
4

Scale Winning Processes

40 min

Once you find what works, learn to scale it. Replicate success across segments, channels, and markets.

You'll be able to

  • Identify patterns in successful automations
  • Template winning sequences for reuse
  • Adapt automations for new segments
5

Team & Resources

30 min

Understand resource requirements for successful automation. Learn when to build in-house vs. partner with experts.

You'll be able to

  • Assess internal capabilities
  • Define roles and responsibilities
  • Make informed build vs. buy decisions
